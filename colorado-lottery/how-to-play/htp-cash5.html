How to Play Cash 5

For just $1, Cash 5 offers a jackpot of $20,000 with drawings every day.

Two Ways to Play

Pick five numbers from 1 to 32 or choose Quick Pick (QP) and the terminal will randomly pick numbers for you.

Never Miss a Drawing

Never miss a drawing by purchasing plays in up to 91 consecutive drawings at once, or 13 weeks in advance. Just check the Advance Play box on the Playslip and let the retailer know how many drawings you want to play. You will play the same numbers for all drawings.

EZ Match

For an extra $1, add EZ Match for a one-time chance to win up to $500 instantly upon check-out. Five EZ Match numbers will be printed below your numbers (that were previously selected or were chosen by Quick Pick). If any of the EZ Match numbers match your numbers, you'll win instantly.

More About Your Ticket

This is how your ticket will look once printed. Your ticket will show your numbers and the range of draw dates for which it is eligible. You will also see EZ Match numbers below the numbers you chose or selected using QP.
