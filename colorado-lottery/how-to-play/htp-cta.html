How to Play Colorado Lotto+

For just $2, Colorado Lotto+ is Colorado's game, with a growing jackpot starting at $1 million that gets bigger until the jackpot gets hit. Drawings are on Wednesdays and Saturdays.

Two Ways to Play

Pick six numbers from 1 to 40 or choose Quick Pick (QP) and the terminal will randomly pick numbers for you.

Check Your Winnings!

Here's what it will look like if you win. Winnings are represented in the payout table on the It's Easy to Play page of the Colorado Lottery website.
