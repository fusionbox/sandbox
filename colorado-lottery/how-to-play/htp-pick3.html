How to Play Pick 3

Pick 3 is a daily game, drawn twice a day, where players pick their own three numbers and choose how to play them.

Pick Your Numbers, and Bet Wager

Select three numbers from 0 through 9. The numbers selected can be three separate numbers or may be duplicated in a single play. For example: '537' or '339' or '777'. You can also choose Quick Pick (QP) to have the terminal pick your numbers for you randomly.

Bet Wagers

Pick 3 is the only Jackpot game from the Colorado Lottery where you can choose how much you want to bet on each play. The more you wager, the more money you can win! Place a wager of 50¢*, $1, $2, or $5 (*minimum $1 wager on Exact Order + Any Order bet type).

Bet Types

The bet type you choose determines how you want your numbers to appear in the drawing. Learn more about the bet types below.

Draw Time

You can select Midday, Evening, or Both.

Never Miss a Drawing

Never miss a drawing by playing up to 28 drawings for just Midday or just Evening drawings. If you play both, you can play up to 56 drawings. The retailer will ask you how many drawings you want to play. You will play the same numbers for all drawings.

More About Your Ticket

This is how your ticket will look once printed. Your ticket will show your numbers and the range of draw dates for which it is eligible. It will also show whether the drawing is for Midday, Evening, or Both. Your bet type will appear next to your numbers along with the wager cost.
